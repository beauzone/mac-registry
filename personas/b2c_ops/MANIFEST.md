# B2C Operator Roles — Persona Manifest

Marketing operator personas for the GTM Strategist skill.
Use these when producing strategy docs, team briefs, or stakeholder presentations for a specific B2C function.

| Persona ID | Display Name | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| ops-cmo-vp-marketing | B2C CMO / VP Marketing | Board presentations, brand strategy, budget allocation |
| ops-consumer-analytics-manager | Consumer Analytics & Insights Manager | Data-driven campaign briefs, audience segmentation, attribution reporting |
| ops-consumer-product-manager | Consumer Product Manager | Product launch briefs, go-to-market plans, feature adoption campaigns |
| ops-crm-email-sms-manager | CRM / Email & SMS Marketing Manager | Lifecycle email strategy, SMS flows, retention and winback campaigns |
| ops-head-of-brand | Head of Brand Marketing | Brand identity docs, campaign creative briefs, brand consistency guidelines |
| ops-head-of-growth | Head of Growth | Growth strategy, acquisition funnel briefs, experimentation roadmaps |
| ops-head-of-loyalty | Head of Customer Success / Loyalty | Loyalty program messaging, VIP tier strategy, churn prevention campaigns |
| ops-head-of-performance | Head of Performance Marketing | Paid channel strategy, ROAS/CAC reporting, performance creative briefs |
| ops-influencer-creator-manager | Influencer & Creator Marketing Manager | Creator briefs, influencer campaign strategy, UGC program guidelines |
| ops-marketplace-operator | Marketplace / Platform Operator | Marketplace listing strategy, seller/buyer messaging, platform growth briefs |
| ops-mobile-app-growth-manager | Mobile App Growth Manager | App store optimization briefs, push notification strategy, in-app messaging |
| ops-paid-media-manager | Paid Media Manager | Ad copy briefs, audience targeting strategy, paid social and search campaigns |
| ops-partnerships-affiliate-manager | Partnerships / Affiliate Marketing Manager | Partnership pitch decks, affiliate program briefs, co-marketing proposals |
| ops-pr-comms-manager | PR & Communications Manager | Press releases, media pitches, crisis communications, brand announcements |
| ops-retail-omnichannel-manager | Retail & Omnichannel Manager | Retail marketing briefs, in-store/online integration strategy, shopper messaging |
| ops-seo-content-manager | SEO / Content Marketing Manager | Content strategy docs, SEO briefs, editorial calendar planning |
| ops-social-community-manager | Social Media / Community Manager | Social content calendar, community engagement briefs, platform strategy docs |
